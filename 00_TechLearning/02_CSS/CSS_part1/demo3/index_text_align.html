Iron Man (Tony Stark)

Iron Man, also known as Tony Stark, is a popular superhero from Marvel Comics. He was created by writer Stan Lee and first introduced in Tales of Suspense in 1963. Tony Stark is a billionaire industrialist, genius inventor, and philanthropist who builds a powered exoskeleton suit, transforming himself into a high-tech superhero.

Origin Story

Tony Stark starts as a wealthy but somewhat reckless weapons manufacturer who experiences a life-changing event when he is captured and seriously injured. To escape captivity and keep his heart beating, he creates a prototype suit of armor. After escaping, he continues to refine the suit, adding advanced technology and weaponry, eventually becoming Iron Man , a force for good.
