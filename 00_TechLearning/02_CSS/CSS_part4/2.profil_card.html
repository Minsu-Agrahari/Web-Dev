Gojo Satoru

Limitless Sorcerer Gojo Satoru is a powerful sorcerer from Jujutsu Kaisen, known for his immense strength and mastery of Limitless and Six Eyes techniques. He is a charismatic mentor with unmatched confidence and skill.

Momo Ayase

Bold Psychic Momo Ayase, from Dandadan, is a spirited high school girl with a strong-willed and courageous personality. She dismisses supernatural phenomena like aliens while firmly believing in ghosts, leading to her humorous and action-packed adventures.
